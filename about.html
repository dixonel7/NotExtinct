Description

The Grant Museum of Zoology hosts a great many extinct species (including fossils and wet specimens) within its walls. However, digital interactions with these specimens is currently unavailable, and museum-goers are sometimes left with questions about what these animals looked and behaved like in real-life. Extinct?! I THINK NOT! Is a mobile app aimed at bringing extinct species to life for curious learners at the museum.

In the museum, visitors are able to use the mobile app to scan each specimen, generating a life-like digital model that demonstrates how these creatures once looked, moved, and sounded. This teaching tool is aimed at teaching visitors about conservation and the importance of protecting endangered species by showing them life-like examples of animals we no longer have the privilege with which to coexist.

Deliverables

The end result will be a mobile app, entitled Extinct?! I THINK NOT! that visitors can download prior to viewing the exhibit.
